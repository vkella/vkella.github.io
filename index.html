About

I am a master's student in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at Arizona State University. I have 4 plus years of personal and educational experience building hardware from scratch, deploying and interfacing hardware & software, and working on sensors. Additionally, I have 2 years of experience as a software engineer, where I worked on developing and maintaining software applications. Currently, I am working as a Research Aide at Arizona State University, where I am a part of a research team. My responsibilities include developing and implementing data visualization tools and applications to support project research and analysis. I have a strong programming background in Python, MATLAB, C, C++. Additionally, I am proficient in Git, Linux, OpenCV, Robot Operating System (ROS).
